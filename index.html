QuickBooks Online AI Accountant

A private, personal-use integration for read-only financial analysis.

This application connects to a QuickBooks Online company via Intuit’s official OAuth 2.0 authorization and the QuickBooks Accounting API. It retrieves financial reports (Profit & Loss, Balance Sheet, Cash Flow, aged receivables/payables, trial balance) and computes metrics, ratios, trends, and screening-level analysis.

Read-oriented & local. The app reads accounting data to produce analysis. OAuth tokens are stored locally on the user’s own machine with owner-only permissions. No financial data is shared with third parties.

Important

This tool provides informational analysis only. It does not file taxes and is not a substitute for a licensed accountant, tax advisor, or auditor.
