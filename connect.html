Connect / Reconnect

This is a local command-line integration. To connect or reconnect a QuickBooks Online company:

  1. Run the application’s login command on your own machine:
    python3 qbo_auth.py login
  2. Your browser opens Intuit’s authorization page. Sign in, choose the company, and approve access.
  3. The app captures the authorization securely via a local loopback redirect and stores the resulting tokens locally (owner-only permissions).

To reconnect after a disconnect or token expiry, simply run the same login command again.
