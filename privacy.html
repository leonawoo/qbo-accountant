Privacy Policy

Application: QuickBooks Online AI Accountant (personal integration)

Last updated: June 21, 2026

1. Overview

This Privacy Policy explains how this private, personal-use application handles

data when connected to your Intuit QuickBooks Online ("QBO") account. The

application is operated by an individual for personal and internal business use

and is not a commercial multi-tenant service.

2. Information We Access

With your explicit authorization via Intuit's OAuth 2.0 flow, the application

accesses financial data from your own QuickBooks Online company, which may

include: company information, accounts, customers, vendors, invoices, bills,

payments, and the financial reports derived from them (Profit & Loss, Balance

Sheet, Cash Flow, aged receivables/payables, trial balance).

The application requests the `com.intuit.quickbooks.accounting` scope. It accesses

data on a read basis to produce financial analysis.

3. How Information Is Used

Accessed data is used solely to display financial reports and compute analysis

(metrics, ratios, trends, valuations) for the authorized user. Data is not used

for advertising, profiling, or any purpose unrelated to the user's own financial

analysis.

4. Storage and Security

  • OAuth tokens (access and refresh tokens) and the company identifier

(`realmId`) are stored locally on the user's own machine, in the application's

configuration directory, with restricted file permissions (owner-only, 0600).

  • Financial data is retrieved on demand for analysis and is not retained in a

central database operated by the application. Any local output remains on the

user's machine under the user's control.

  • Client credentials (Client ID/Secret) are stored locally and never transmitted

except to Intuit's official OAuth endpoints.

5. Data Sharing

The application does not sell, rent, or share your financial data with any

third party. Data is exchanged only between the user's machine and Intuit's

official API endpoints. No analytics, tracking, or third-party data processors are

used.

6. Data Retention and Deletion

Tokens persist only until the user disconnects. The user may revoke access at any

time via QuickBooks Online (Apps → Disconnect) or by deleting the local token

file, which immediately ends the application's access. Upon disconnection, stored

tokens are invalidated and may be deleted.

7. Children's Privacy

The application is not directed to children and does not knowingly collect data

from anyone under 16.

8. Intuit Relationship

This application uses Intuit's APIs but is not endorsed by or affiliated with

Intuit Inc. Your use of QuickBooks Online is also governed by Intuit's own privacy

policy and terms.

9. Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated; the "Last updated" date will reflect changes.
