Disconnect

You can disconnect this application from your QuickBooks Online company at any time:

  1. From QuickBooks Online: go to Settings → Apps (or the Intuit account’s connected-apps page), find this app, and choose Disconnect.
  2. From the application: run python3 qbo_auth.py revoke, which revokes the refresh token with Intuit and deletes the local token file.

Upon disconnection, stored access and refresh tokens are invalidated and the application can no longer access your data.
